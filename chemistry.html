Chemistry

Organic, Inorganic, and Physical Chemistry

Chapter 1: Atomic Structure

The fundamental structure of matter.

1.1 Subatomic Particles

  • Protons: Positive charge, in nucleus
  • Neutrons: No charge, in nucleus
  • Electrons: Negative charge, orbit nucleus

1.2 Atomic Number and Mass

Atomic Number = Number of Protons
Mass Number = Protons + Neutrons

Chapter 2: Periodic Table

Organization of chemical elements.

2.1 Groups and Periods

Vertical columns are groups (families), horizontal rows are periods.

2.2 Periodic Trends

  • Atomic radius decreases across a period
  • Electronegativity increases across a period
  • Metals on left, non-metals on right

Chapter 3: Chemical Bonds

How atoms combine to form compounds.

3.1 Ionic Bonds

Transfer of electrons between metals and non-metals.

3.2 Covalent Bonds

Sharing of electrons between non-metals.

3.3 Metallic Bonds

Sea of electrons in metal structures.
