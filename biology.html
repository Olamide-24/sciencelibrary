Biology

Cell Biology, Genetics, Ecology, and more

Chapter 1: Cell Structure

The cell is the basic unit of life.

1.1 Cell Theory

  • All living things are made of cells
  • Cells are the basic unit of structure and function
  • All cells come from pre-existing cells

1.2 Cell Organelles

  • Nucleus: Control center, contains DNA
  • Mitochondria: Powerhouse, produces ATP
  • Ribosomes: Protein synthesis
  • Cell membrane: Selective barrier

Chapter 2: Genetics

The study of heredity and variation.

2.1 DNA Structure

Double helix structure with nucleotide bases: A, T, G, C.

A pairs with T, G pairs with C

2.2 Mendel's Laws

  • Law of Segregation: Alleles separate during gamete formation
  • Law of Independent Assortment: Genes assort independently

Chapter 3: Ecology

Interactions between organisms and environment.

3.1 Ecosystem Components

  • Producers: Plants (autotrophs)
  • Consumers: Animals (heterotrophs)
  • Decomposers: Bacteria and fungi

Energy flow through trophic levels.
